Shipping Information

Shipping Options & Delivery Times

At StyleNest, we strive to get your orders to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. We offer various shipping options to meet your needs.

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
  • Cost: Rs 350 (Free for orders over Rs 10,000)
  • Available for all domestic orders.

Express Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
  • Cost: Rs 800
  • Expedited service for faster delivery.

International Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 7-21 business days (varies by destination)
  • Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.
  • Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Processing

  • Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Important Notes

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., weather, customs delays).
  • Please ensure your shipping address is accurate to avoid delays.
  • For any shipping-related inquiries, please contact our customer support.
